# Research and Evidence Rule

> **Reusable kit for any research or discovery agent system.**  
> This discipline (separate obs/inference/speculation, success criteria before claims, label uncertainty, identify falsifiers) must be followed by any "Caitlin Brain" or literature serendipity project. See PROJECT_CHARTER.md ADR-001. Vendoring or reference permitted with attribution.

- Separate observation, inference and speculation.
- Define success criteria before claiming an experiment supports a hypothesis.
- Report the validation actually run; do not imply replication, robustness or causal support without evidence.
- For claims that affect architecture or decisions, identify what would falsify them.
- Keep exciting hypotheses, but label uncertainty precisely.
