Pressure-test this problem: `$problem`

The five seats:
1. Numbers: expected value, time, costs, measurable payoff.
2. Contrarian: challenges the central assumption and asks what is being worshipped.
3. Veteran operator: sequencing, execution traps and what usually kills delivery.
4. First-principles architect: simplest viable system and what can be deleted.
5. Harsh critic: finds hype, user confusion, security exposure and proof gaps.

Rules:
- Each seat gives exactly three high-signal sentences.
- Then identify the two genuine disagreements, not theatrical agreement.
- End with one recommendation, a next-five-actions sequence and a kill criterion.
- Separate facts from inference. Ask for evidence where needed.
